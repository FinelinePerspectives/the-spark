Frequently Asked Questions

Are utilities included in the rent?

Utilities are not included.

What appliances are included?

Stove, fridge, microwave, dishwasher, washer & dryer.

Are there balconies?

Select units have private balconies.

Do you offer furnished suites?

Not at this time.

Is there parking available?

Parking is available for an extra fee per month. Please reach out to our leasing team for more information.

Is there bike storage available?

Yes, secure bike storage is available.

Are there storage lockers?

Storage lockers are first come first serve. Rentable for an extra fee per month.

Is the building pet friendly?

Yes, pets up to 30 lbs are allowed.

Is the building smoke free?

Yes, the building is entirely smoke free.

What amenities are included?

Common room, bike room & lobby.

When will move-ins begin?

May 2023.

Is tenant insurance required?

Yes, tenant insurance is required to rent at The Spark.
